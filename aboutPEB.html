About PEB

PEB, or Pre-engineered buildings, are elements of a building that are pre-made in factories. However, the assembly usually takes place on the site itself. They are usually distinct from conventional structural steel buildings. Hence, they are often regarded as an amazing alternative. Depending on the design, these will usually be made in the factory itself and then transported to the site directly.

While there are individuals under the misconception that structured steel might be more expensive, in reality that might be a myth. The initial investment might seem to be difficult to manage and more expensive in comparison to the other building materials. However, such is the durability and its low maintenance cost that it often outweighs the initial costing. We do recognise the need for making PEB with structured steel more affordable. If you calculate the overall costs, PEB will be much more affordable over a longer period of time as the associated costs are much lesser than traditional building materials.
